=== Section 4: State transitions ===
Start                       : status=DRAFT, canEdit=true
After DRAFT->IN_REVIEW      : status=IN_REVIEW, canEdit=true
After IN_REVIEW->SCHEDULED  : status=SCHEDULED, canEdit=false
After SCHEDULED->PUBLISHED  : status=PUBLISHED, canEdit=false
After PUBLISHED->ARCHIVED   : status=ARCHIVED, canEdit=false

Rejection path:
After IN_REVIEW->DRAFT      : status=DRAFT, canEdit=true

Cancel schedule:
After SCHEDULED->DRAFT      : status=DRAFT, canEdit=true

Invalid transitions (each throws):
DRAFT->PUBLISHED            : cannot transition from DRAFT to PUBLISHED
DRAFT->ARCHIVED             : cannot transition from DRAFT to ARCHIVED
PUBLISHED->DRAFT            : cannot transition from PUBLISHED to DRAFT
ARCHIVED->PUBLISHED         : cannot transition from ARCHIVED to PUBLISHED
